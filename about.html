We are glad that you would like to know more about Devranjani Music School - lets know what the School has to offer!

Classical Music & Classical Dance are the main forte of Devranjani. Music is taught just like the way a Subject is taught in any academic school. The school follows a well structured Syllabii as laid down by well known Music Colleges. Equal emphasis is given to Practical & Theoretical aspects of Music. Another important part of Pedagogy is the stage shows in which students participate with full zeal & enthusiasm.

Unique thing about the school is the wide repertoire of Music subjects taught :

Indian Classical

  • Vocals
  • Kathak Dance

Western Classical

  • Piano
  • Electronic Keyboard
  • Acoustic Guitar

Devranjani takes the beauty of Music to people of all age brackets - from 6 to 80 years.
While the elderly ones enjoy Music at Devranajni which keeps them going, the young ones at Devranjani learn the basics of Music & clear Music Examinations from World Known Music Universities. See more on this in our Associations page.
Here, Music is not kept confined within the four walls of the school.
Everyone at Devranjani gets a number of opportunities to showcase his Music talent on stage. The Teachers really work hard to prepare the performers for various events.
