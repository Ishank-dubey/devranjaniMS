About Devranjani Music School, Jaipur

An Institution dedicated to classical Music -Indian and Western. London College of Music and Bhatkhande Music Univ Lucknow for long term Music and dance Courses.

We teach Indian Vocal, Guitar, Electronic Keyboard, Piano and Kaththak dance to Students 6 yrs upwards. Certification is from Nationally and Internally acclaimed Music Univ. Teaching is done by professionally qualified Faculty in a very stimulating yet serene atmosphere.

Devranjani Music School prepares Kids for Long term Music Certification Examinations since the year 2003. The Institution is known for churning our Toppers every year. Today the Centre has grown multifold from 1 Kid - 1 Teacher Combination.

Devranjani Music School was founded by Mrs. Sarita Dwivedi in the year 2003, she has a Masters in Music and has vast experience in Indian Vocal and Western Instruments like Keyboard and Piano.
