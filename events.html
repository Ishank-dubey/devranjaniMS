Events By Devranjani Music School

Music & Dance are a Performing art, so what is learnt at Devranani is taken out & showcased at best of the auditoria of Jaipur. All Concerts organized by Devranjani Music School are always based on a Theme. This has lent an image of Off-beat Shows to all Programmes of Devranjani.

Crescendo

The much awaited annual Concert for all Learners at Devranjani !
Crescendo is being celebrated every year since 2012. As the name suggests, this Event has grown in popularity among student fraternity every year. Crescendo offers the right platform to students for showcasing their Music talent.
All performances , whether in Vocals or Dance or Instrumental, are properly backed by the best of Accompanists around. Preparation for Crescendo begins quite in advance so as to deliver the best.

Malhar

A Concert to welcome the arrival of Monsoon in India !
Held during the Saawan month of Hindu Calendar every year, students celebrate the beginning of Rainy Season in India with Indian Raagas & Kathak Dance Performances.

Vintage Years

This event celebrates the spirit of being a Golden Ager. Every member of Super-60s Club eagerly look forward to this Event. There is a sudden eruption of Childhood instincts in all Super-60s on the day when the Theme of ‘The Vinrage Years’ is announced.
It is conducted every year on 1st October – the International Day for Elders.

Ada-E-Ghazal

The subtle art of Ghazal singing is celebratedonce a year through Ada-E-Ghazal Concert. Members of Jaipur Ghazal Club conduct this with lot of enthusiasm.
