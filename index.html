Music is always in the air , all around us, the world is full of Music. It requires only a thin vent for music to surge out to enshroud everything which comes in its way.

And Devranjani Music School perfectly fits into that role of small vent of Music.
Whoever comes under its wings never wants himself to decouple. That is the real power of Music !

Everything about the school - the serenity surrounding the premises, the tranquil ambience inside and well qualified & stable Faculty all are simple, yet mesmeric!

The Frequencies & the Pitches produced by a Musical Harmony leave an indelible impact on Human Psyche.
One can observe such magic of Keys & Chords at Devranjani. A hyperactive child gradually starts behaving in a disciplined & controlled manner. And when no one is around with an Elderly one , Music becomes his best companion.

So, wherever on earth you are , do join Devranjani Family and explore yourself the beauty that Music has to offer for each one of us.
