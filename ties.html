Students of Devranjani Music clear exams conducted by various World Known Music Universities

Devranjani Music School strictly follows a Syllabus in whatever we teach. For Indian & Western Classical Music we have tie-ups with World’s leading Music Institutions.

Bhatkhande Sangeet Vidyapeeth, Lucknow

For Indian Vocals & Kathak Dance we follow the Pedagogy recommended by Bhatkhande Sangeet Vidyapeeth Lucknow.
Music Theory & Practical Exams are given to students once a year. The passing out students get Degrees of Prathma, Madhyama, Vishaarad & Nipun.

London College of Music

Devranjani Music School is Preparation-cum-Examination Centre for Graded Music Examinations conducted by London College of Music. University of West London conducts its International Music Examinations through its constituent College - London Lollege of Music. Students learn the art of playing Instruments Piano, Electronic Keyboard & Acoustic Guitar.

Examiners come from abroad to give test to students once a year.
Passing out students get Certificates of Grade Initial to Grade VIII. These Certificates are awarded by University of West London.
These Certificates are helpful for students in getting admission in U.K based Colleges after XII Class.
Aside from these, Devranjani Music School is also Preparation Centre for International Graded Music Examinations of Trinity College , U.K.
