Devranjani Music School is not confined only to preparing Kids for International Graded Music Exams.

A firm believer in Out-of-the Box thinking process , Devranjani has taken Music to those who need it for tonning up their stressfull life.

Super-60s

A novel Music Club of Senior Citizens ! The Club was launched on 1st October 2017 during a high octane Music Concert at Jaipur. Males & Females of above 60 years’ age are members of this coherent family of Senior Citizens. They enjoy the Music in their monthly get togethers with Accompanists are at hand. In a year they conduct multiple Stage Shows as well. Music has become a panacea for all the members . It keeps them going while setting aside their age related minor irritants. Over the years club’s appeal has grown. This is reflected in the craze other Senior Citizens have for its membership.
A real masterpiece of Devranjani Music School curation!

Jaipur Ghazal Club

Ghazal, in itself, is an independent School of Vocal Music. Not all Singers can sing a Ghazal nor do all audience like this genre. The ardent Ghazal Lovers of Jaipur have come together to form Jaipur Ghazal Club. The members have their Ghazal Mehfils regularly. They also have their Ghazal Shows 2/3 times in a year.
Again, a subtle illustration of Devranjani Creativity!

Super-Moms

An Ode to Motherhood !
Mothers are so tied-up with their family chores that their Hobbies & Interests never get a place on their dashboard. But Music always have a very strong Pull effect. Super-Moms is a real testimony to the lasting impression that Music leaves on someone. Once floated, the idea of Super Mom – the Music Club, was immediately lapped up by those Mothers who always wanted to escape the rerun of their daily regimen. The budding Musician Mothers enjoy old & new Bollywood Melodies in their fortnightly get togethers.
